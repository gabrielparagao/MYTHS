Myths of the New Age  

Request the first chapter of the book and begin the journey to healing and enlightenment.

Order

Gods you've met

And yet may not believe.

Events you know

Now you can live through.

Places you recognize

But has never been.

Times in our 

own history

Yet we were not there.

A Mother

And the impossible mission of being one.

A Father

And all the mistakes we've made.
