The universe is too big. But we might just be alone. 

In the quest for conscience we became the most precious thing in the universe.

Conscience is the gift. Even the Gods wonder our potential 

Not many of them could witness the miracle of the human mind. 

Have you ever wondered why? She did too.

And she has fought to find out. She has died for our sins, been reborn from ash, basked in the enlightenment of answers.

Maybe it is the answer you seek as well. It was for me. 

Writing this book I have found the answers I seeked for so long, and raised questions that I am okay never answering.  

The art is what we mean. The meaning is what we're for.

This is what we've been waiting. The feeling of what is missing, is another side of the will to live.

The resolution we desire. Is not what we want at all.

We can only experience what we live, eternity is not the answer we seek, feeling is.
